***-- Authentic Momos --***

Our love for momos is beyond words, this Tibetan specialty has been one of the most popular street foods in India and other Asian countries. One can easily find a momo vendor on every nook and corner of the street if they want to relish a hot and steamy street food on a cold winter night. However, if you are worried about hygiene, we have a solution for you. Momo is a simple and healthy snack recipe that you can easily prepare at your home. Made with delectable vegetable and chicken stuffing, it is served with a variety of special sauces that are spicy and even sweet. Here, we have selected a few authentic momo recipes that can be enjoyed anytime you crave for this delectable dish.
